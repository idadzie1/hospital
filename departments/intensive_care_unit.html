Our Intensive Care Unit

Our state-of-the-art ICU is equipped with the latest medical technology, including ventilators for respiratory support, patient monitors for vital sign tracking, infusion pumps for precise medication delivery, and dialysis machines for renal support. This allows our highly skilled team to provide the most advanced and effective care for patients with critical illnesses.
